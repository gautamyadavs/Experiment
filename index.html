How electives work

You will choose five elective courses to tailor the MSLE program to your interests, background, and goals.

At least three of your five electives must come from at least three different program dimensions shown below. It is your responsibility to make sure your electives satisfy this distribution requirement.

Program dimensions

Important details
  • Cross-listed electives count toward only one program dimension.
  • Elective offerings vary by academic year and are not guaranteed every year.
  • Each elective must be the equivalent of a full semester course, usually 9 or 12 units. Two mini courses of 6 units each may count together as one elective.
  • Electives that are not listed in the approved course list must be individually approved by the program director on a case by case basis, based on the student’s goals and future plans.

*05-898 and 05-899 Special Topics in HCI feature varying topics by section and semester; some topics may be offered only once or twice.
